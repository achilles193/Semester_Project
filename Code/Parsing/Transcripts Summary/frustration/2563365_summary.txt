ABSTRACT / SUMMARY
Client discusses the two relationships she is in and the difference between the men. One of the men is more of a father figure and there is a strong sexual desire between them, but he's married and the client is unsure if he will ever leave his wife. The other man seems to be really open and caring, and the client doesn't feel like she deserves this.
