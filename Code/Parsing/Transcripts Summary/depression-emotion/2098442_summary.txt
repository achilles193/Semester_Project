ABSTRACT / SUMMARY
Client fears disappointing her mother if she cannot complete her dissertation this year. She feels lonely and disliked sometimes; she is one of the only single, childless females in her social circle and often feels left out. She is embarrassed about her obsessive-compulsive behaviors and realizes that she is the one who has withdrawn from many of her relationships because of it.
